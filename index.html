Are we in LOCKDOWN?

NO

But from 14 March 2020:

Ban on all recreational events, sports, culture and other.
Restaurants, bars are closed.
Schools are closed, but daycare stays open. Schools need to provide daycare for children without other solutions
All non essentail shops are closed during the weekend

At least that is the Belgian situation, more countries will be added in the future.

How to minimize the risk of infection?

Wash your hands thorough with soap.
Keep at least 1m distance between you and the others.
Wipe down doorhandles and other objets with alcohol wipes.
Cover your mouth and hands with a paper towel.
Avoid groups
